Do's
•	Think before posting. Ask if it reflects your professional image.
•	Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts and profile.
•	Tag credits/sources for images, quotes, or ideas you share.
•	Set time limits (e.g., 30 mins/day) to balance studies and scrolling.
•	Report/block cyberbullying, scams, or inappropriate content immediately.
Don'ts
•	Share personal details like address, phone, or exact location.
•	Post during class time or exams. (stay focused on studies)
•	Accept friend requests from unknown people without verification.
•	Share screenshots of private chats or confidential group work.
•	Use offensive language or argue publicly. (handle privately)
